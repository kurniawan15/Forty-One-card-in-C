	Rules of The Game
	-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
    	1. 41 is a partnership game is normally played by from 2 to 4 players.
    
    	2. Deal randomly four cards face down to each player.  
    
    	3. The remaining cards are placed face down in the centre of the table to form a drawing stock.

	4. The turn to play passes clockwise.

	5. On the first turn of the game, the first player can draw only from the stock, 

	   because no cards have yet been discarded.

	6. The next turn, draw either the top card of the stock or the card just discarded by the previous player.

	7. Add this card and discard one card face up.
	
	8. The only discard that can be taken is the card just discarded by the player before.

	9. Repeat step six and seven until the winner was found or there are no cards left in the drawing stock.

	10. If there are no cards left in the drawing stock, all players show their cards and count their scores.


	Scoring
	------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

	1. Each player can only count cards of one suit as positive, and cards of other suits have a negative value.
	
	2. Aces count 11 points, pictures (kings, queens and jacks) and tens count 10 points each, 

	   and cards from 2 to 9 count face value.

	3. The perfect score is Fourty-One (41).

	4. The player with the highest score is the winner and the one with the lowest score is the loser.
